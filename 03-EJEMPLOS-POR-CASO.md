## Podcast

### Intro de podcast
```
A warm, energetic female host introduces a true crime podcast.
Confident delivery with slight dramatic pause before the title.
Short punchy music sting, subtle studio ambience, transition swoosh effect.
Host voice centered and clear, music low, clean mastering.
```

### Segmento de entrevista
```
Podcast interview segment with two speakers.
Host (male, curious, engaged): "So what happened next?"
Guest (female, reflective, measured): "That's when everything changed."
Studio ambience, soft background music bed, clean dialogue.
```

### Outro de podcast
```
Warm closing of a storytelling podcast episode.
Narrator (calm, reflective): "And that's where our story ends... for now."
Music swells gently, soft fade out, studio ambience fades last.
Music leads, voice fading, clean ending.
```

## Radio Drama

### Drama de suspenso
```
Radio drama scene in a late-night convenience store.
Two characters:
- Guard (male, bored, tired): "We're closing in five minutes."
- Customer (female, nervous, whispering): "I know. I just need a moment."
Ambient: fluorescent hum, distant traffic, fridge buzz, clock ticking.
Music: low suspenseful drone building tension.
SFX: door bell chime, footsteps on tile, freezer door opening.
Dialogue prominent, music very low, ambient subtle.
```

### Drama romántico
```
Romantic radio drama scene at a rainy bus stop at dusk.
Character A (male, gentle, hesitant): "I've been waiting for you."
Character B (female, warm, surprised): "I didn't think you'd come."
Ambient: rain on pavement, distant city traffic, occasional car passing.
Music: soft piano melody, melancholic and hopeful.
SFX: rain drops, umbrella opening, footsteps in puddles.
Voice warm and close, music supporting, rain ambient.
```

### Drama de terror
```
Horror audio drama in an abandoned hospital.
Single character (female, terrified, breathing heavy):
"Hello? Is anyone there?"
Ambient: empty hallway revent, distant echoes, creaking metal.
Music: dissonant strings, rising tension.
SFX: footsteps on broken glass, door slamming in distance, whispers.
Dialogue center, effects punctuated, music building dread.
```
